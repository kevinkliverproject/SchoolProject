No Water to drink?
Resource scarcity has become a big problem and the reason are we human.

The result of overpopulation is that many resources are not available.
The problem with the food. For example poorer countries have problems with food. The reason for that are many factors, these are for example the weather, the climate and poverty. Poverty can be caused by corruption and poor governance.

The problem with the water. But also the scarcity of water is getting a huge problem in many countries. The resaon for that are population, economic growth and chaning consumption habits. Besides there are only 3% drinkable water at the whole, called the freshwater. The other 97% are salt water and that is undrinkable.

The Problem with the wood. Also the wood is getting fewer. Especially the deforestation is a huge factor. That is when people cut down trees in a large area, also animals get killed by that and their living space is getting destroyed.

We are using resources as if we had two planets, not one. There can be no 'plan B' because there is no 'planet B.'
